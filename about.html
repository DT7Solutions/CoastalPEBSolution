About Our Company

Four decades of Excellence

Coastal PEB Solutions is a leading provider of pre-engineered building solutions in India. We offer various services, including design, engineering, fabrication, and installation of PEBS and other building solutions Coastal PEB Solutions, founded by childhood friends Mr. Shiva Shankar Rao and Mr. Ramesh, has an inspiring journey that traces back to 1983 when they established the company under the name Coastal Engineering Works. Drawing inspiration from Shiva Shankar Rao's father's welding shop during 1960’s, they initially crafted PEBS through manual construction methods. Over time, their dedication and innovation led them to automate their processes, progressively embracing full automation.
