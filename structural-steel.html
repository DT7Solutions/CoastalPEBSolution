Structural Steel

Coastal PEB boasts extensive expertise in designing, manufacturing, and distributing a diverse array of robust steel structures. These structures are meticulously crafted using a variety of materials including Welded Beams, Hot Rolled Sections, Parallel Beams, Angles, Beams, Channels, Pipes, and Hollow Tubes. Leveraging cutting-edge software like STAAD PRO, AUTOCAD, ETABS, AUTODESK REVIT, and TEKLA STRUCTURES, their engineering team employs their profound knowledge to create optimized steel structures with utmost precision.

Coastal PEB Solutions is a leading provider of pre-engineered building solutions in India.
